Easy Air Fryer Pork Chops

How to Make Delicious Air Fried Pork Chops

Start by mixing all your dry ingredients in a shallow dish. Take your parmesan, paprika, garlic powder, salt, parsley and pepper. Coat the pork chops with olive oil and then cover in the seasoning mix. It's best to cook 2 chops at a time and flip them halfway through the cook time. Cook time will be roughly 10 minutes at 380 degrees F.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 (5 oz) boneless pork chops
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil

Directions

  1. Preheat the air fryer to 380 degrees F (190 degrees C)
  2. Combine parmesan cheese, paprika, garlic powder, salt, parsley, and pepper in a shallow dish and mix well.
  3. Coat each pork chop with olive oil and cover both sides of the pork chop in the mixture.
  4. Put 2 pork chops in the air fryer and cook for 10 minutes, flipping halfway though. Transfer to a cutting board and let them rest for 5 minutes. Complete this step with the rest of the pork chops.
  5. Serve and Enjoy
